ABOUT US

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store. There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.

TOPTRACER RANGE

Golfers can enjoy a fun and interactive practice session with Toptracer Range, as it also offers various game modes and virtual golf courses to hone their skills in an engaging way.

ADVENTURE GOLF

Adventure golf, also known as mini-golf or putt-putt, is a leisurely and entertaining form of golf typically played on creatively designed miniature courses. It often features imaginative obstacles, themed landscapes, and challenging putting scenarios, making it a fun activity for families and friends.

GOLF LESSONS

Golf lessons are personalized coaching sessions designed to improve a golfer's skills, technique, and overall performance on the course. Professional golf instructors provide guidance on swing mechanics, club selection, and course management, tailoring lessons to individual strengths and weaknesses.
